# Who We Are For

TEHNIKI is strongest for buyers who need technical depth and structured execution, not random feature delivery.

## Best-Fit Clients

### Founders With a Live Product

The product exists, but backend logic, operations, or system structure are starting to limit growth.

### Teams With a Fragile Backend

The team can keep shipping, but every change creates too much risk.

### Businesses Needing Operational Systems

Important business processes still depend on manual coordination, fragmented tooling, or poor control visibility.

### Teams Preparing to Scale

The next phase requires stronger architecture, reporting, and operational clarity before new growth can be supported.

### Products That Need Technical Direction

The project needs real engineering judgment, not only implementation capacity.
