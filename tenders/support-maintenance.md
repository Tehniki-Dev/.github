# Support and Maintenance

Some systems need a delivery partner for a phase.
Some need ongoing technical continuity.

## Support Modes

- post-phase support
- stabilization support
- maintenance after launch
- continued platform expansion

## Suitable Scenarios

- live systems with ongoing operational risk
- products entering growth after stabilization
- platforms where backend ownership must continue across phases
- systems that need a technical partner rather than isolated feature delivery

## Goal

Support should extend system stability and visibility, not create dependency through chaos.
