# Company Profile

## Overview

TEHNIKI is an engineering studio for backend systems, operational platforms, workflow automation, and AI-enabled business products.

It was built by founders with hands-on operational and engineering experience.

We work on systems where product interfaces, backend logic, data, integrations, billing, reporting, and operational control all matter at the same time.

Founding Team:

- TEHNIKI founders stay close to product, delivery, and technical direction

## What We Are Best At

- backend and product platforms
- operational systems and internal control layers
- workflow automation and AI-enabled business systems
- architecture and stabilization work
- analytics, reporting, and optimization systems
- Telegram-connected products when they sit on top of a real backend or operating flow
- specialized infrastructure-aware product lines

## Delivery Scope

We design, build, deploy, and support web applications from MVP foundations to production systems.

Typical scope can include:

- product interfaces
- backend systems
- databases and data layers
- infrastructure and deployment workflows
- phased post-launch support and expansion

## What Makes TEHNIKI Different

We are not positioned as a generic outsourcing team.

Our strongest zone is delivery for products that:

- have real operational weight
- need strong backend ownership
- require structure before scale
- depend on integrations, workflows, billing, or reporting
- need technical direction, not only task execution

## Delivery Style

We prefer:

- clear system boundaries
- phased execution
- curated delivery when client-side coordination needs to stay lighter
- controlled growth instead of chaotic feature shipping
- audit-first entry when risk is high
- maintainable product logic over short-term patching

## Best-Fit Work

We are strongest when entering products through:

- MVP build with a serious technical core
- post-MVP stabilization or expansion
- architecture recovery before growth
- analytics or optimization layer buildout
- operational system design
