# What We Do Not Do

This page exists to make the TEHNIKI working model clear.

## We Do Not Enter Blind

We do not promise serious delivery without understanding the system state, business pressure, and technical context.

## We Do Not Push Rewrites Without Reason

If the system can be improved through phased cleanup or controlled restructuring, that is usually better than a blind rewrite.

## We Do Not Treat Operational Systems Like Disposable Prototypes

If the product depends on workflows, support, reporting, billing, planning, or operator control, those parts matter.

## We Do Not Optimize for Feature Volume Over System Stability

Shipping more does not help if the product becomes harder to operate, extend, or trust.

## We Do Not Position Ourselves as a Generic Team for Everything

We prefer to be clear about where we are strongest and where a project is not the right fit.

## Why This Matters

These boundaries are part of how we protect:

- delivery quality
- client clarity
- system maintainability
- long-term product value
